Some insight to Gary Larson's life and career

  • Gary Larson created the popular comic series "The Far Side".
  • He was born and raised in University Place, Washington which is a suburb of Tacoma.
  • He has resisted the use of his comics on the internet, which makes this project a bit ironic
  • Larson has inspired generations of artists and comics
  • The Far Side can be found on over twenty books as well as popular calendars
